Guide to KhanCrypt

To encrypt: Type your message into the message textbox and create a secret passphrase. Click encrypt to get the encrypted message.

To decrypt: Copy/paste your encrypted message into the message textbox and enter your secret passphrase. Click decrypt to get the decrypted message.

This progam uses the Advanced Encryption Standard (AES) cipher algorithm through CryptoJS. AES is a symmetric-key algorithm which has been adopted by the U.S. government in 2001. Since then, AES protects everything from bank transactions to classified information. For more general information about AES, visit the Wikipedia page.
